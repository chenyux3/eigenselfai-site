This site is the public-facing home of EigenSelf — a generative framework for
self-understanding. It is the place where writing about the framework will live:
short essays, excerpts, notes on what the reports are doing under the hood, and
reflections from readers who choose to share theirs.

The reports themselves are delivered privately. Nothing personal about any
individual reader is published here.

More writing is on its way.
